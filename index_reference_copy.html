Hopper Window

Morris Aguilar

I reverse-engineer painters' compositional logic into parametric 3D code. Hopper's windows are not incidental — they are the subject: a frame within a frame, depth rendered as emotional distance. This scene derives every proportion from a single parametric chain and renders in real time via Three.js.
